The Road Travelled

2015 — Present

Publisher & Editor-in-Chief

Urban Express News
● Current Role

Founded and leads Urban Express News — Nigeria's bold digital platform dedicated to urban development journalism, investigative reporting, and social justice advocacy. Responsible for full editorial direction, content strategy, team management, and daily publishing operations. The platform reaches hundreds of thousands of monthly readers across Nigeria.

Visit urbanexpresslive.com ↗
2010 — 2015

Senior Reporter & Correspondent

The Nation Newspaper

Specialized in investigative reporting on urban governance, land rights, and infrastructure policy at one of Nigeria's flagship broadsheet newspapers. Received editorial commendation for an in-depth exposé on Lagos property documentation bottlenecks that prompted a legislative review. Covered the Lagos State government's urban planning policies with analytical depth.

2005 — 2010

Staff Writer & Urban Affairs Reporter

PM News

Wrote extensively on Lagos State affairs, local government policy, and community impact stories. Built a reputation for ground-level reporting that amplified marginalized voices, particularly those affected by housing insecurity, transport policy changes, and environmental degradation in Lagos' rapidly expanding peri-urban areas.

2001 — 2005

Reporter & Feature Writer

Commoner Newspaper

Specialized in community journalism, bringing the lived experiences of ordinary Nigerians to print. Developed foundational skills in field reporting, source cultivation, and investigative fact-finding. This period shaped a deep commitment to working-class and grassroots storytelling that defines his journalistic identity to this day.

1998 — 2001

Staff Journalist

ThisDay Newspapers

Launched his professional career at one of Nigeria's most prestigious and internationally recognized newspapers. Built core skills in hard news writing, long-form feature journalism, editorial standards, and source management under the rigorous editorial culture of one of Africa's most respected newsrooms.
